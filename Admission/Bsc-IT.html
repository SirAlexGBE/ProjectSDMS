BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineering (IT)

The BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineeringat ISMT is delivered through BTEC HND and from the University of Sunderland, UK. The curriculum covers all key aspects of computing and IT, with specific focus in software engineering, networking, Data Technology, AI, Android Mobile Development and information systems. It is aimed at those students who have strong interests in information technology and wish to acquire knowledge in the application of computing and IT in a range of industries and businesses.
