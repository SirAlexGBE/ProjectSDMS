BSc (Hons) Cyber Security and Digital Forensics

The programme is designed to enhance students with knowledge and applied skills in cybersecurity and digital forensics through lab exercises, case studies, and project work. They will learn how to analyze and assess vulnerabilities and risks, develop strategies to mitigate these threats and investigate cybercrime incidents using digital forensics techniques. Our aims to produce highly skilled and professional graduates with abilities in resolving computer forensic problems and cases, preserving the evidential integrity in computer forensic analysis, developing and managing computing solutions, understanding the legal, social, ethical and professional responsibilities of computer forensics ractitioners and having a broad awareness of the computing industry.
